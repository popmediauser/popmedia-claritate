# Pop Media Social-First Promotion Research

Date: 2026-06-24
Scope: whether Pop Media should keep client/sponsor content primarily on social media and use the website as a clean proof and contact hub. No publishing, no outreach, no automation.

## Recommendation

Use a social-first model now.

The website should not host every client, every video, every file, or every campaign. It should present:

- the offer;
- selected proof;
- official channel links;
- the types of content delivered;
- contact.

The social channels should carry the active content:

- reels;
- posts;
- carousels;
- stories;
- audio snippets;
- client tags;
- campaign recaps.

## Why This Is Better Now

Belgium has a strong social media base. DataReportal's 2026 Belgium report says Belgium had 9.08 million social media user identities in October 2025, equal to 77.2% of the population. This supports using social channels as the active distribution layer.

The site should stay simple because Google recommends people-first content and warns against content created mainly to manipulate search. For Pop Media, that means: clear offer, clear proof, no filler.

The current Pop Media product is not a heavy platform. It is:

- story;
- voice;
- community;
- distribution;
- simple reporting.

That fits social media better than a large hosted archive.

## What To Add To The Website

Add one compact section before contact:

**Canale + dovada**

This section should say:

- Pop Media uses social channels for live distribution;
- the client receives approved posts/reels/stories;
- the website links to official channels and selected examples;
- campaign results are reported with real numbers only.

Do not add:

- fake follower numbers;
- fake client logos;
- fake testimonials;
- embedded social feeds;
- a dashboard;
- many client pages;
- a public archive of all files.

## Social Content Model

For one client:

1. Reel or short video: 15-45 seconds.
2. Carousel: offer, location, service, contact.
3. Story: tag, reminder, link.
4. Audio snippet: 15-30 seconds from the interview or spot.
5. Website/social link: one clear destination.
6. Recap: reach, clicks, visits, messages, and client feedback if available.

## Disclosure And Trust Rules

If there is money, barter, free product, discount, invitation, commission, or any other benefit, the post must be treated as advertising.

Use clear labels in Romanian:

- `Publicitate`
- `Reclama pentru [nume firma]`
- `Parteneriat platit cu [nume firma]`
- `Segment sponsorizat de [nume firma]`
- `Listare sponsorizata`

Avoid using only vague labels:

- `colaborare`
- `partener`
- `ambasador`
- `multumim`
- `sustinut de`
- `#ad`
- `#spon`

Put the disclosure at the beginning of the post, on the first screen of the video/story, or through the platform's paid partnership label when available.

## Current Official Channel Signals

Radio Belgia's own website links to:

- Facebook;
- Instagram;
- YouTube;
- TikTok.

These should be linked from the Pop Media page as official channels, but exact follower/reach numbers should only be added after checking the accounts directly and recording the date.

## Website Copy Recommendation

Short section title:

> Canale si dovada

Short paragraph:

> Campaniile traiesc pe social media, unde oamenii vad, distribuie si interactioneaza. Site-ul ramane locul curat unde prezentam produsul, canalele oficiale si rezultatele confirmate.

Proof blocks:

- `Social`: postare, reel sau story aprobat;
- `Audio`: spot sau fragment din poveste;
- `Link`: QR, WhatsApp sau canal social;
- `Raport`: reach, clickuri, vizite si feedback.

## Decision

Add one compact section to the homepage and keep the operation manual for the first clients.

Automate later only after:

- 3-5 paying clients;
- confirmed content workflow;
- real social metrics;
- clear permission process;
- repeated reporting needs.
