# SEO Content Length Research

Date: 2026-06-24
Scope: Pop Media static homepage, Romanian-language business presentation, no new visual sections in this step.

## Core Finding

There is no magic SEO word count.

Google explicitly warns against writing to a preferred word count. The goal is not to make the page longer; the goal is to make it complete enough for people and clear enough for search engines.

For Pop Media, this means:

- avoid filler;
- keep the page scan-friendly;
- use the first screen to explain the business value;
- make sponsor benefits concrete;
- add more words only when they answer a real visitor question.

Current page snapshot:

- visible page text: about `286` words;
- current title length: `37` characters;
- current meta description length: `102` characters.

This is already close to a good lightweight homepage. The safest target is not "much more text"; it is `320-450` visible words after copy optimization.

## Recommended Word Ranges For This Site

These are working ranges, not strict SEO rules.

| Site part | Target length | Why |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Browser title | 45-65 characters | Descriptive, concise, less likely to feel vague. |
| Meta description | 120-160 characters | Enough to explain radio, community, sponsors, Belgium. |
| H1 | 3-8 words | Strong identity, easy to scan. |
| Hero paragraph | 25-45 words | Explain who it is for and why it matters. |
| Primary CTA | 2-4 words | Action should be obvious. |
| Navigation labels | 1-2 words each | Fast scanning; no marketing language. |
| Service card copy | 12-24 words each | Short, specific, no fluff. |
| Sponsor intro | 35-65 words | Explain the buyer benefit before packages. |
| Package names | 2-4 words each | Name the outcome, not the tier. |
| Package bullets | 3-5 bullets, 2-5 words each | Buyers compare quickly. |
| Contact copy | 20-40 words | Give one simple next step. |
| Full homepage | 320-600 words | Enough context for SEO without turning into an article. |

## Practical SEO Rules For Pop Media

### 1. Header / Hero

The hero should answer three questions quickly:

1. What is this? Radio/media for Romanians in Belgium.
2. Who is it for? Community, listeners, sponsors, event partners.
3. What benefit does a business get? Visibility and trust in Romanian-language community contexts.

Recommended hero copy target:

- H1 stays emotional: `Vocea romanilor in Belgia`.
- Paragraph becomes more concrete:
  - 30-40 words;
  - include `radio`, `podcasturi`, `evenimente`, `romanii din Belgia`, `promovare in romana`.

### 2. Services

Keep five services if they are already visually stable. Do not add more.

Each service should say what the visitor gets, not just what Pop Media does.

Example:

- Bad: `Dăm voce brandului tau.`
- Better: `Transformam mesajul afacerii tale in radio, social media si continut usor de distribuit.`

### 3. Sponsor Section

This is the most important commercial part.

Current issue:

- `Basic / Plus / Premium` is easy to understand internally, but weak for a visitor.

Better names:

- `Vizibilitate locala`;
- `Interviu si continut`;
- `Eveniment si comunitate`.

Why:

- the package name says the outcome;
- the buyer can self-select faster;
- no extra design needed.

### 4. Contact

Do not add a fake form.

Use one direct CTA and make the copy specific:

- "Scrie-ne ce vrei sa promovezi si iti raspundem cu o propunere simpla."

## What Not To Do

- Do not add text only to hit a number.
- Do not repeat `romani in Belgia` unnaturally.
- Do not make every section equal in importance.
- Do not add fake claims, fake metrics, fake testimonials, or fake sponsor logos.
- Do not create technical public copy like `static demo`, `backend`, or `integrare live`.

## Source Grading

Tier 1 / official:

- Google Search Central says SEO is about helping search engines understand content and helping users decide whether to visit.
- Google says helpful content should serve people first, and specifically warns against writing to a supposed preferred word count.
- Google title guidance recommends descriptive and concise titles, and warns against keyword stuffing.
- Google meta description guidance recommends unique, descriptive, human-readable summaries.

Tier 2/3 / usability and SEO tooling:

- Nielsen Norman Group research says people scan web pages and prefer concise, scannable, objective writing.
- Yoast's public guidance treats word count as a quality floor, not a ranking guarantee, and suggests regular pages usually need at least about 300 words to avoid thin content.

## Step-By-Step Implementation Plan

### Step 1: Header / Hero Only

Make only invisible SEO head updates plus the visible hero copy and CTA.

Change:

- title;
- meta description;
- brand note if needed;
- nav CTA;
- hero paragraph;
- hero CTA labels.

Do not change:

- layout;
- spacing;
- hero image;
- wave;
- service cards;
- sponsor packages.

### Step 2: Services

Keep the same layout and icons. Tighten card text only.

### Step 3: Sponsor Packages

Rename packages by benefit and tighten bullets. No new section.

### Step 4: Contact

Make the contact text more specific. No fake form.

### Step 5: Technical SEO

After copy feels right:

- canonical URL;
- Open Graph tags;
- sitemap;
- robots;
- optional JSON-LD only with verified business details.
