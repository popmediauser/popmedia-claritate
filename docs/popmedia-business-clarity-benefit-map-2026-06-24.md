# Pop Media Business Clarity / Benefit Map

Date: 2026-06-24
Scope: business clarity for Pop Media / Radio Belgia public presentation. No UI changes in this pass.

## One-Sentence Business Definition

Pop Media is a Romanian-language community media channel in Belgium that helps people discover Romanian stories, events, and services, while helping local businesses become trusted and visible inside that community.

## What Pop Media Should Not Try To Be Yet

- Not a big media platform with accounts, dashboards, and complex features.
- Not a generic agency that sells every type of marketing.
- Not a directory of all Romanian services in Belgium; that is closer to the info.belgia lane.
- Not a page full of sections just because each asset exists.

The simple public story should be:

1. We have a Romanian community media voice.
2. We create audio/content/events around that community.
3. Businesses can sponsor or promote through this trusted context.
4. Contact us for a clear package or a custom collaboration.

## Audiences

### 1. Romanian Community / Listeners

They need:

- familiar language;
- useful local stories;
- event discovery;
- Romanian culture and connection;
- trust, not corporate noise.

What they should feel:

- "Aici ma regasesc."
- "Aflu ce se intampla in comunitate."
- "Pot asculta sau descoperi evenimente fara complicatii."

### 2. Sponsors / Local Businesses

They need:

- visibility to a specific audience;
- trust transfer from a community voice;
- simple packages;
- examples of deliverables;
- an easy next step.

What they should feel:

- "Aici pot ajunge la romanii din Belgia mai natural decat prin reclame reci."
- "Inteleg ce primesc."
- "Pot incepe cu un pachet mic, fara risc mare."

### 3. Event Organizers / Partners

They need:

- event awareness before the event;
- presence during the event;
- recap after the event;
- sponsor visibility.

What they should feel:

- "Pop Media poate transforma un eveniment intr-un moment de comunitate, nu doar intr-un afis."

## Public Benefits Worth Saying

Use these benefits because they are clear and believable without fake numbers.

### Benefit 1: Vizibilitate in limba romana

Businesses do not only need reach; they need to be understood. Romanian-language promotion is useful for services where trust, explanation, and local context matter.

Good public copy:

> Promovare in romana, pentru oameni care inteleg mai repede cand mesajul vorbeste pe limba lor.

### Benefit 2: Incredere prin comunitate

Pop Media can position sponsors inside familiar formats: radio, interview, event, recap. This is warmer than a cold ad.

Good public copy:

> Nu apari doar ca reclama. Apari intr-un context de comunitate, langa povesti, evenimente si oameni reali.

### Benefit 3: Continut care se poate refolosi

One collaboration can produce multiple assets:

- radio mention;
- short audio spot;
- interview;
- social clip;
- event announcement;
- post-event recap.

Good public copy:

> Dintr-o singura colaborare putem crea materiale care se folosesc la radio, online si la evenimente.

### Benefit 4: Legatura intre offline si online

Romanian businesses often need local recognition, not only clicks. Pop Media can connect:

- radio/audio;
- social media;
- event presence;
- direct contact.

Good public copy:

> Combinam vocea radio cu vizibilitatea online si prezenta la evenimente.

### Benefit 5: Claritate pentru antreprenori ocupati

Many small businesses do not want a marketing lecture. They want to know: what do I get, how long it takes, and how I start.

Good public copy:

> Alegem impreuna obiectivul, pregatim mesajul si iti spunem concret ce livram.

## Offers That Add Value Without Noise

### Offer A: Pachet Start / Vizibilitate Locala

For small businesses that want to be known.

Includes:

- short radio mention or audio spot;
- one social post or short clip;
- simple message polish;
- recap with links/materials.

Benefit:

- "Ma vede comunitatea fara sa investesc intr-o campanie mare."

### Offer B: Interviu / Poveste De Brand

For entrepreneurs, artists, organizers, service providers.

Includes:

- interview or podcast conversation;
- headline/story angle;
- short clips for social;
- linkable recap.

Benefit:

- "Oamenii inteleg cine sunt si de ce pot avea incredere."

### Offer C: Eveniment / Comunitate

For events, fairs, concerts, cultural days, stands, sponsors.

Includes:

- announcement before event;
- radio/social mentions;
- optional media presence;
- recap after event;
- sponsor visibility.

Benefit:

- "Evenimentul traieste inainte si dupa ziua in care se intampla."

### Offer D: Partener Lunar

For businesses that need repeated recognition.

Includes:

- monthly radio mentions;
- one interview/feature per period;
- event or social tie-in when relevant;
- monthly recap.

Benefit:

- "Nu apar o singura data si dispar. Construiesc prezenta."

## What To Remove From The Public Story

- Generic claims like "solutia perfecta", "parteneriat strategic", "crestem impreuna" without explaining how.
- Too many service labels in one row if they do not lead to action.
- Any fake metrics, fake sponsor logos, fake testimonials, or "numarul 1".
- Technical labels like "static demo", "backend", "integrare live".
- Contact choices that look like a form but do not actually submit.

## What To Add First On The Existing Page

This is intentionally small.

1. Keep the current hero visual.
2. Change only the hero paragraph to clarify the business:
   - "Radio, podcasturi si evenimente pentru romanii din Belgia. Pentru afaceri, cream promovare in romana, in contexte de comunitate."
3. Change the sponsor section from `Basic / Plus / Premium` to benefit names:
   - `Vizibilitate locala`
   - `Interviu si continut`
   - `Eveniment si comunitate`
4. Change the sponsor paragraph:
   - "Alegem mesajul potrivit si il ducem in formate simple: radio, social, interviu sau eveniment."
5. Add no new large section until these copy changes feel right.

## Suggested Page Hierarchy Without Adding Noise

1. Hero: emotional identity + one plain business sentence.
2. Existing services: radio, podcasts, events, promotion, community.
3. Stories: keep as proof of content formats.
4. Sponsor packages: rename by benefit and make deliverables concrete.
5. Contact: one clear invitation.
